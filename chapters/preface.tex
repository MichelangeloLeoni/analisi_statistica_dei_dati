\chapter*{Preface}
These notes are intended to be a comprehensive introduction to the field of statistical data analysis, 
covering both theoretical foundations and practical applications through examples, exercises, and tests. 
The material is organized into chapters that  follow the \textit{Analisi statistica dei dati} course at the univeristy of Pisa,
followed by the authors during the 2024-2026 academic years. It is by no means an exhaustive treatment, but we hope to leave the readers
with a solid understanding of the core concepts and techniques in statistical data analysis, as well as the ability to apply them to exam-style problems.

Any errors, typos, or suggestions for improvement are very welcome. Please feel free to add a pull request or reach out to us directly via email.

We wish you the best of luck in your studies!
